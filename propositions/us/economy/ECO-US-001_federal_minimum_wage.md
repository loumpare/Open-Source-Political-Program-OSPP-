## Summary

Raise the federal minimum wage from $7.25 to $17/hour, indexed to inflation thereafter.

## Proposal

Increase the federal minimum wage to $17/hour, phased in over 3 years (+$3/year), then indexed annually to CPI. States and localities may set higher floors.

## Estimated impact

- Workers affected: ~33 million (EPI, 2025)
- Wage increase for median affected worker: +$6,000/year
- CBO estimated job impact: -200k to +400k (wide confidence interval)

## Arguments for

- Lifts millions above poverty line without increasing taxes
- Stimulates consumer spending in low-income communities
- Reduces reliance on federal assistance programs

## Arguments against

- Risk of job losses in low-margin sectors (fast food, retail)
- One-size-fits-all ignores cost-of-living differences (rural vs NYC)
- May accelerate automation
